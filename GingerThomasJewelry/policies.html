Financing

We offer financing options to help you with your purchases.

1. Monthly Payment Plans

We offer convenient monthly payment options with terms ranging from 3 months to 12 months. With this plan, you can spread the cost of your jewelry purchase over time, while enjoying your new piece today.

  • 0% APR for 3 to 6 months for qualified buyers.
  • Low-interest rates starting from 9.99% APR for terms between 9 and 12 months.
  • Flexible payment schedules (weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly).

2. Layaway Program

Our Layaway Program allows you to reserve a piece of jewelry by making a deposit and paying the balance over time before taking the item home. This option is great for customers who prefer to pay over time without taking on interest.

  • Deposit: 20% of the total price due at the time of purchase.
  • Payment Period: Up to 3 months to complete the balance.
  • No interest or finance charges as long as payments are made as agreed.

Delivery

1. Delivery Methods

We offer several delivery options to suit your needs. Choose the method that works best for you at checkout.

  • Standard Shipping: Delivery within 5-7 business days. Free for orders over $50.
  • Expedited Shipping: Delivery within 2-3 business days. Available for an additional fee.
  • Overnight Shipping: Guaranteed next-day delivery. Available for an additional fee.

2. Shipping Costs

Shipping fees vary depending on the shipping method and the destination of your order. Shipping costs will be calculated at checkout.

3. International Shipping

We currently offer international shipping to most countries. Delivery times and shipping costs may vary depending on the destination. Please note that international orders may be subject to additional customs fees, taxes, or duties, which are the responsibility of the customer.

4. Processing Time

All orders are processed within 1-2 business days. Orders placed on weekends or holidays will be processed the next business day.

5. Order Tracking

Once your order has shipped, you will receive a tracking number via email so you can track your package in real-time.

6. Delivery Issues

If you experience any issues with your delivery, please contact our customer service team at support@gingerthomasjewelry.com for assistance.

Return Policies

1. Return Eligibility

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you are not happy with your order, we offer a return within 30 days of receipt. To qualify for a return, items must be in new, unworn condition with all original tags and packaging intact.

2. Non-Returnable Items

For hygiene reasons, certain items are non-returnable. This includes custom-made pieces, earrings, and any items that have been worn or altered after delivery. Please ensure you review your order carefully before making a purchase.

3. How to Return an Item

To initiate a return, please contact our customer service team at support@gingerthomasjewelry.com with your order details. Once your return is approved, we will provide you with instructions on how to return the item, along with a return authorization number.

4. Return Shipping

Customers are responsible for the return shipping costs, except in the case of defective or damaged items. We recommend using a trackable shipping service to return your item. Ginger Thomas Jewelry is not responsible for lost or undelivered return packages.

5. Refunds

Once your return is received and inspected, we will process your refund to the original payment method. Please note that it may take 5-10 business days for the refund to reflect on your account, depending on your payment provider.

6. Exchanges

We do not offer direct exchanges. If you would like to exchange your item for a different size or style, please return the original item for a refund and place a new order for the item you'd like to receive.

7. Damaged or Defective Items

If you receive a damaged or defective item, please contact us immediately at support@gingerthomasjewelry.com with a description and photos of the damage. We will gladly assist you with a replacement or refund for damaged goods at no additional cost to you.
